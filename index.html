About Us

Startup Expo is a premier platform for innovative startups to present their ideas, secure funding and gain valuable insights from industry experts delivering an experience worthy of a cinematic masterpiece. The event orchestrates a seamless blend of opportunities and resources, setting the stage for entrepreneurial success. With over 50 promising startups participating, the Expo has firmly established itself as a cornerstone of the entrepreneurial ecosystem. Previous iterations have featured an impressive lineup of 100+ investors, incubators and venture capitalists, facilitating unparalleled investment and networking opportunities. The upcoming season of the Startup Expo is set to continue this illustrious legacy, offering startups the center stage to captivate audiences, secure pivotal opportunities and excel in their entrepreneurial journey.

In the previous iteration, Startup Expo witnessed collaborations with prominent government organisations such as the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Software Technology Parks of India (STPI), Madhya Pradesh Startup Centre, and more. The event offered extensive networking and investment opportunities to over 50 startups by connecting them with 100+ investors, incubators, bureaucrats and venture capitalists, with investment firms including 100X.VC, Kalaari Capital, Unicorn India Ventures, TiE India Angels, Nasscom, Indian Accelerator, and participating startups securing soft commitments exceeding INR 18 crore. Notably, Alchemyst AI raised INR 2.5 crores from Inflection Point Ventures, CSID Technomatrics secured INR 1.2 crore government tender and INR 20 lakh incubation grant, Lucid Implants raised INR 80 lakhs, and The Upthrust successfully secured INR 16 crores in investment. The previous editions have featured renowned personalities, such as Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh, Dr. Mohan Yadav Ji, Ashneer Grover, Tanu Jain, and others, alongside prestigious sponsors like the Adani Group.

Who can Participate?

  • Startups must be registered with their active website.

  • Startups should be in scaling phase- incubation, seed funded or pre-seed stage.

  • Startups must have 1-2 years of active revenue generation.

Registration Process

  1. Selected startups will be notified through email and phone after the review and selection process.

  2. Following the shortlisting process, startups must confirm their participation by sending an email to E-Cell MANIT Bhopal at expo@ecellnitb.org .

  3. Subsequently, each selected startup will be assigned a unique slot number and a point of contact from the E-Cell team.

  4. Upon confirmation, startups are required to complete the payment process to E-Cell MANIT within 3-days. The booth allocation fee is INR 10,000 for a booth of 8√ó8 sq ft, which includes banners, posters, 2 chairs, a table, and an electric socket for convenience.

Screening Process

1 Pitching sessions will be held in front of investors, followed by visits to individual booths, where investors may engage with promising startups whose ideas resonate with their interests.

2 A panel of judges, comprising investors, mentors and selected members from E-Cell NIT-B's partner organisations, will evaluate the startups based on their pitching. Based on their assessments, startups will be awarded cash prizes or credits.
