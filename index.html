About Us

The Startup Expo is an expedition to pitch your ideas, secure funding, and establish a basecamp in the expertise-driven cliffs of entrepreneurship. It provides a platform for 50+ promising startups, and has been the catalyst for soaring achievements. In the past, it has provided extensive panel sessions and networking opportunities with over 100+ Angel Investors and Venture capitalists. With an impressive legacy, it continues to be the quintessential launchpad for startups, offering valuable opportunities to propel them to new heights.

Who can Participate?

  • Startups should be registered, with their active websites.

  • Startups should be in scaling phase- incubation, seed funded or pre seed stage.

  • Startups should be actively generating revenue for 1-2 years.

Registration Procedure

  1. Selected startups will be notified through email and call, following the reviewing and selection process.

  2. After selection, startups must confirm their participation by sending an email to E-Summit MANIT Bhopal at startupexpo@ecellnitb.in.

  3. Each of the chosen startups will receive a unique slot number and a point of contact from the E-Summit team.

  4. Within three days of confirmation, startups should complete the payment process to E-Summit MANIT. The booth allocation fee is INR 10,000 for a booth size of 8Ã—8 sq ft inclusive of banners and posters, as well as 2 chairs and a table equipped with an electric socket for convenience.

Screening Process

1 Pitching will be done in front of investors, followed by their visit to individual booths, where they may interact if the startup idea generates interest in them.

2 A Judging panel composed of investors, mentors, and a few members of E-Cell NIT-B's partners will give points to the startups upon their pitching, according to which they will be given cash prizes or credits.
